## A time to settle the mind, on your way home from work

Sabai Mind NPO will hold a beginner-friendly **8-session meditation course** at the **Wat Phra Dhammakaya Tochigi Branch** in Utsunomiya, starting May 26, 2025.

The sessions begin at 19:30 every Tuesday — perfect to drop in on the way home from work. An experienced Thai monk will guide the sessions in Japanese.

## Curriculum

The 8 sessions are organized in three steps — **Settle → Use → Integrate** — so you can build from the basics of meditation to applying it in daily life.

- 1,500 yen per session (tax included)
- Single-session and walk-in attendance welcome
- About 20 participants per session

> Note: This is not a religious or recruiting event.
