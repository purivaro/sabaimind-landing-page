## A vibrant celebration

On July 20, 2025, Sabai Mind held the Songkran Festival 2025 in Tochigi to a wonderful turnout.

Songkran, the traditional Thai New Year, carries the meaning of washing away the misfortunes of the past year and purifying body and mind through the playful splashing of water. Under clear skies, members of the local community, the Thai community, and people of all ages came together.

## Highlights

- Thai food stalls and community gathering
- Blessings and dhamma talks by monks
- The water-pouring purification ritual
- Traditional Thai music and dance

A heartfelt thank you to everyone who joined and to all our volunteers.

We'll continue to hold the Songkran Festival each year as part of Tochigi's summer tradition.
