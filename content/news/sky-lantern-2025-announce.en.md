## A single wish, into the night sky

On Sunday, October 12, 2025, Sabai Mind NPO will hold the Sky Lantern Festival 2025.

The moment of lighting a lantern and entrusting a single wish to the night sky — a Thai tradition of gratitude and reverence to the Buddha. We hope you'll join us at **Thai Meisou no Mori** in Tochigi.

## Event Details

- **Date & Time**: Sunday, October 12, 2025 — 17:00 to 19:00
- **Venue**: Thai Meisou no Mori (1021-1 Sorihata, Nasukarasuyama, Tochigi)
- **Admission**: Junior high & up: 2,000 yen / Elementary & under: 1,000 yen
- **Sky Lantern**: 2,000 yen each (reservations recommended)

The venue will have authentic Thai food stalls, dhamma talks, and blessings from Thai monks. Bring someone special and share an autumn evening with us.
