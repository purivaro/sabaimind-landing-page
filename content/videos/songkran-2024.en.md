## A day of cleansing through water

Sabai Mind held the traditional Thai New Year festival "Songkran" in Nasukarasuyama, Tochigi.

Family and friends gather to splash water on one another, washing away the misfortunes of the past year. Watch the day unfold — Thai food stalls, blessings from monks, and traditional music — in this video.
