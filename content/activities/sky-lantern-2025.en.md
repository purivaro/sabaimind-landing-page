## Event Overview

The Sky Lantern Festival is a special moment when people light lanterns, send wishes to the night sky, and express gratitude and reverence to the Buddha. Rooted in Thai tradition, this festival takes place at "Thai Meisou no Mori" in Tochigi.

## Program

- Authentic Thai food stalls
- Dhamma talk and blessing by Thai monks
- Releasing sky lanterns together into the night sky
