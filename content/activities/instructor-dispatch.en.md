## Service Overview

We dispatch Thai monks who have trained in meditation in Thailand for many years to anywhere in Japan. We tailor each engagement flexibly to schools, companies, hospitals, and community events.

> **We never proselytize.** Please use this service with peace of mind.

## Where We Can Serve

- Schools — heart and ethics classes
- Companies — mental health and training programs
- Hospitals and welfare facilities — care programs
- Community events
- Various seminars and workshops

## Languages

Our dispatched monks can teach in multiple languages:

- Japanese
- English
- Chinese
- Thai

## Cost

**Actual travel expenses + a voluntary offering.** No fixed amount — flexible by case and consultation.

We have resident monks in the Kantō, Kōshin'etsu, Chūbu, and Kinki regions, allowing for lower travel and lodging costs.

## How to Request

1. Get in touch by form or email
2. Discuss the content, date/time, and venue
3. On the day, the monk arrives at your location

Consultation and quotes are free. Please feel free to reach out.
