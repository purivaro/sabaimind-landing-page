## Tuesday evenings — won't you pause for a moment?

Daily life and work keep our minds racing. Before we know it, the day ends without our minds ever truly resting.

This meditation circle is a place to ease that tension and spend quiet time once a week. It is structured so you can join directly after work, in a way that's easy to keep up with.

## Who it's for

- Your mind never stops racing
- You want to shift gears emotionally
- You want time to truly relax

An experienced Thai monk guides the session in gentle Japanese. Even if it's your first time meditating, you can start with the basics — posture, breath — at your own pace.

> Note: This is not a religious or recruiting event.

## 8-Session Program — Three Steps

### Settle (Sessions 1-2) — Build the foundation

- **Session 1** ｜ Posture & breath: the foundation of meditation
- **Session 2** ｜ Concentration with words (mantra meditation)

### Use (Sessions 3-6) — Cultivate mind and concentration

- **Session 3** ｜ Working with the mind without being swept by thoughts
- **Session 4** ｜ Focus that flows (Flow state)
- **Session 5** ｜ The sense of returning to your center
- **Session 6** ｜ What happens in the mind and brain during meditation

### Integrate (Sessions 7-8) — Make it part of daily life

- **Session 7** ｜ A peaceful mind in relationships
- **Session 8** ｜ Bringing meditation into daily habits
