## About Songkran

Songkran is the traditional Thai New Year. Family and friends gather to splash water on each other, washing away the misfortunes of the past year and welcoming the new year with a clean heart.

## Songkran 2025

On July 20, 2025, Sabai Mind held the Songkran Festival in Tochigi. The Thai community, local residents, children and adults — many came together for a vibrant celebration.

- Thai food stalls
- Blessings by Thai monks
- The water-pouring purification ritual
- Traditional Thai music and dance

Thank you to everyone who joined us.
